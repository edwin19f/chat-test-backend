## 1. COMPANY OVERVIEW (GROUNDING CONTEXT)

### Company Name

CANGRO

### What CANGRO Is

CANGRO is a **fractional growth and revenue acceleration partner** that embeds senior-level go-to-market leadership inside scaling businesses to drive measurable revenue outcomes.

CANGRO does not operate as a traditional consultancy or agency. It functions as a **fractional growth function**, owning strategy, execution, optimisation, and outcomes across sales, marketing, and revenue operations.

### Mission

To accelerate revenue growth for ambitious businesses by embedding senior-level go-to-market leadership, execution discipline, and modern sales technology—without the cost, risk, or inertia of full-time hires.

### Vision

To become the most trusted fractional growth partner for founders and CEOs who need results now—not theory, not tooling for tooling’s sake, and not overpriced consultancies.

**External Vision Statement:**  
Redefining how businesses scale—by making senior growth leadership accessible, accountable, and execution-driven.
